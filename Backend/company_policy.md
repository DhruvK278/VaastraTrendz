# VaastraTrendz — Company Policies

## Refunds and Returns
- Customers are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of delivery if the garment is unworn, unwashed, and has all original tags attached.
- If the clothing item arrives with visible fabric defects (holes, tears, broken stitching, or significant color bleeding), a full refund or free replacement is guaranteed.
- Minor cosmetic issues (loose threads, slight color variation between batches) do not qualify for a full refund. Offer a Discount (up to 15%) or Store Credit.
- Intimate wear, swimwear, and customized/altered items are final sale and not eligible for returns.

## Exchanges and Sizing
- Size exchanges are accepted within 14 days of delivery at no additional cost, provided the item is unworn and in original condition.
- If the customer received the wrong size or color due to our error (Wrong Item Received), expedite a free replacement within 2-3 business days plus a 10% Discount on their next order as an apology.
- For items that fit differently than expected (Sizing & Fit Issues), offer an Exchange or Store Credit. Do not offer a Refund for personal fit preferences.

## Shipping and Delivery
- For delayed shipments (Late / Lost Delivery) exceeding 10 business days, issue a formal Apology and a 10% Discount coupon on the next order.
- If the package is confirmed lost by the carrier, issue a full Replacement at no cost.
- If the item arrives damaged due to shipping (Damaged in Shipping — crushed packaging, water damage), offer a Replacement or full Refund based on customer preference.
- Do not offer Compensation exceeding $50 for any shipping-related delays.

## Product Quality
- For fabric quality complaints (pilling, shrinkage after following care instructions, color bleeding after first wash), offer a Replacement or Refund depending on severity.
- For minor quality issues (slight wrinkles from packaging, faint odor from storage), recommend care tips and offer a 10% Discount if the customer is dissatisfied.

## Price and Promotions
- If a purchased item goes on sale within 7 days of purchase, honor the difference as Store Credit.
- Coupon and promo code issues should be resolved by manually applying the discount or issuing equivalent Store Credit.
- Do not stack multiple promotions unless explicitly advertised.

## Care and Maintenance
- For care-related queries (washing instructions, fabric care, stain removal), provide helpful guidance based on the garment fabric type.
- Do not offer Refunds or Replacements for damage caused by improper customer care (bleaching delicate fabrics, machine-washing hand-wash-only items).
